Explore the public middle schools

Here you can explore the different school districts and boroughs.
A the first map you can investigate the school districts, while the boroughs can be explored in the second map.
In the left side of the maps you can choose which variable you want to explore and the areas will be colored from low to high.
You can also zoom in and explore which schools are located in each area.
You are welcome to change the color of the mpa if you have a preference - this is done in the bottom in the left hand side.
